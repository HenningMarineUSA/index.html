Welcome to American Boat Racing

About Us

Henning Marine USA brings the adrenaline of African boat engineering to American waters. Our South African-built boats are designed for speed, safety, and high-performance water sport competition. Backed by decades of racing, our mission is to grow a nationwide movement in boat racing across the United States.

Our Boats

Every Henning Marine boat is crafted with precision, using advanced composite materials and tuned for wave-crushing performance. U.S. Coast Guard compliant and ABYC-certified, these boats are ready for race day or rugged recreation.

USA Racing League

Join our national racing league! Represent your state, wear your colors, and compete in epic water battles. The Henning Marine USA League partners with local clubs, sponsors, and the national watersport federation to bring elite-level racing coast to coast.
